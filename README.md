# 🏙️ NagpurLens

### Real Estate Intelligence Platform for Nagpur

> Transforming fragmented real estate data into actionable investment intelligence.

---

## 📖 Overview

NagpurLens is a city-scale real estate intelligence platform designed to analyze, visualize, and interpret real estate growth patterns across Nagpur, Maharashtra.

The platform integrates property market trends, infrastructure development indicators, demographic insights, and locality-level price benchmarks into a unified analytical framework.

Using SQL, Python, and Power BI, NagpurLens helps identify:

- Emerging growth corridors
- Undervalued investment zones
- Infrastructure-driven appreciation patterns
- Locality-level market intelligence
- Data-backed investment opportunities

### Mission

> Make real estate decisions data-driven rather than speculation-driven.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Nagpur is undergoing one of the fastest urban transformations in Central India, driven by infrastructure and economic development initiatives such as:

- MIHAN Expansion
- Nagpur Metro
- Ring Road Development
- Smart City Projects
- Industrial and Logistics Growth

Despite this rapid development, real estate decisions remain largely dependent on:

- Broker recommendations
- Word-of-mouth information
- Fragmented datasets
- Limited market transparency

### Key Challenges

| Challenge | Impact |
|------------|----------|
| Lack of consolidated data | Investors struggle to identify growth opportunities |
| Speculative decision-making | Increased investment risk |
| No integrated intelligence platform | Information remains fragmented |
| Delayed awareness of growth zones | Buyers enter after prices have already appreciated |

NagpurLens addresses these challenges through a data-driven intelligence framework.

## 🗄️ Database Architecture

NagpurLens uses a relational database model designed specifically for locality-level real estate intelligence and investment analysis.

### Core Tables

| Table | Purpose |
|---------|----------|
| Localities | Master table containing locality-level information and geographic references |
| Properties | Property pricing, market activity, and real estate indicators |
| Infrastructure | Metro stations, MIHAN influence, Ring Road access, and connectivity metrics |
| Amenities | Schools, hospitals, shopping centers, and lifestyle infrastructure |
| Investment_Scores | Computed investment attractiveness scores and ranking outputs |

### Entity Relationship Overview

```text
Localities
│
├── Properties
├── Infrastructure
├── Amenities
└── Investment_Scores
```

# 🏆 Investment Attractiveness Score (IAS)

The core innovation behind NagpurLens is the proprietary:

## Investment Attractiveness Score (IAS)

A weighted scoring framework that ranks every locality on a scale from **0–100**.

### Scoring Methodology

| Factor | Weight |
|---------|---------|
| Price Growth Rate | 35% |
| Market Activity Score | 25% |
| Infrastructure Score | 30% |
| Amenities & Livability Score | 10% |

### Objective

Provide a simple, interpretable, and data-backed framework for comparing investment opportunities across Nagpur.
